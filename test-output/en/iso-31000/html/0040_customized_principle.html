Purpose

This document describes the "Customized" principle of ISO 31000 risk management. The risk management framework and process are aligned with the external and internal context of the organization.

Principle Description

The risk management framework and risk management process are customized and proportionate to the external and internal context of the organization related to its objectives.

Key Statements

  • Risk management considers organizational context
  • Approach is proportionate to risks and objectives
  • Methods are aligned with organization size
  • Flexibility for different situations
