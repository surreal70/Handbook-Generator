Purpose

This document describes the monitoring and review of risk management according to ISO 31000.

Monitoring Objectives

  • Ensure effectiveness of risk management
  • Identify changes in risk profile
  • Evaluate treatment measures
  • Detect new risks
  • Continual improvement

Monitoring Levels

Strategic Level

  • Organization's risk profile
  • Achievement of risk management objectives
  • Appropriateness of framework

Operational Level

  • Individual risks and their development
  • Implementation of treatment measures
  • Effectiveness of controls
