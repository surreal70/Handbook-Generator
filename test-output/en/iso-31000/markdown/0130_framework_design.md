## Purpose

This document describes the design of the risk management framework according to ISO 31000 Clause 5.5. It defines how the framework is adapted to the organizational context.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- AdminSend GmbH
- Risk management framework design
- All organizational levels
- Internal and external stakeholders

## Design Principles

The risk management framework must be tailored to the organization and consider its external and internal context, risk management commitment, and resources.

### Design Factors

1. **Organizational Context**
   - External factors (market, regulation, technology)
   - Internal factors (culture, structure, processes)
   - Stakeholder expectations
   - Organizational objectives and strategy

2. **Risk Management Commitment**
   - Top management engagement
   - Risk management policy
   - Risk appetite
   - Resource commitments

## Framework Components

### 1. Understanding the Organization and its Context

**External Context Factors:**
- Regulatory environment
- Market conditions
- Technological developments
- Social and cultural factors
- Competitive situation

**Internal Context Factors:**
- Organizational structure
- Corporate culture
- Capabilities and resources
- Information systems
- Stakeholder relationships

### 2. Articulating Risk Management Commitment

**Commitment Elements:**

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Policy | Risk management principles |
| Objectives | Strategic and operational objectives |
| Risk Appetite | Acceptable risk levels |
| Responsibilities | Roles and authorities |

### 3. Assigning Responsibilities and Authorities

**Governance Structure:**
- Board: Oversight and strategic direction
- Risk Management Committee: Coordination and monitoring
- Risk Manager: Implementation and support
- Department Heads: Risk management in departments
- Employees: Risk awareness and reporting

**Responsibility Matrix:**

| Role | Responsibility | Authority |
|------|---------------|-----------|
| CEO | Overall responsibility | Strategic decisions |
| CRO | Risk management coordination | Framework design |
| Department Heads | Departmental risks | Measure approval |
| Employees | Risk reporting | Escalation |

### 4. Allocating Resources

**Resource Types:**

1. **Personnel Resources**
   - Risk management team
   - Subject matter experts
   - Training resources

2. **Financial Resources**
   - Risk management budget
   - Measure financing
   - Insurance

3. **Technical Resources**
   - Risk management software
   - Data analysis tools
   - Communication systems

4. **Information Resources**
   - Risk databases
   - Best practices
   - External information

### 5. Establishing Communication and Reporting

**Communication Plan:**

| Audience | Content | Frequency | Method |
|----------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Board | Strategic risks | Quarterly | Report |
| Management | Operational risks | Monthly | Dashboard |
| Employees | Risk awareness | Ongoing | Newsletter |
| Stakeholders | Material risks | Annually | Report |

**Reporting Structure:**
- Risk register
- Risk dashboards
- Management reports
- Stakeholder communication

## Design Process

### Phase 1: Analysis
- Understand organizational context
- Identify stakeholders
- Determine requirements
- Research best practices

### Phase 2: Conception
- Define framework structure
- Design processes
- Establish roles and responsibilities
- Determine resource needs

### Phase 3: Validation
- Align framework with stakeholders
- Check feasibility
- Ensure resource availability
- Obtain approval

### Phase 4: Documentation
- Document framework
- Create policies
- Describe procedures
- Develop training materials

## Design Criteria

### Effectiveness
- Framework achieves risk management objectives
- Risks are appropriately treated
- Decision quality improves

### Efficiency
- Appropriate resource utilization
- Avoidance of duplication
- Automation where possible

### Adaptability
- Framework can adapt to changes
- Scalability for growth
- Flexibility for different risk types

### Acceptance
- Framework is accepted by employees
- Simple application
- Clear benefits visible
