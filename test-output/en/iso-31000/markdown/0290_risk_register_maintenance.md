## Purpose

This document describes the maintenance and updating of the risk register.

## Update Process

### Regular Updates

- Monthly review of all active risks
- Update of assessments
- Status updates on treatment measures
- Documentation of changes

### Ad-hoc Updates

- Upon occurrence of risk events
- Upon significant context changes
- Upon new insights
- Upon completion of treatment measures

## Data Quality

### Quality Criteria

- Completeness of information
- Currency of data
- Consistency of assessments
- Traceability of changes

## Archiving

- Completed risks are archived
- Historical data available for analyses
- Lessons learned documented
