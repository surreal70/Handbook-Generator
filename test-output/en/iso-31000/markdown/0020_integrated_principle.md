## Purpose

This document describes the "Integrated" principle of ISO 31000 risk management. Risk management is an integral part of all organizational activities.

## Principle Description

Risk management is not a stand-alone process but part of the purposes, governance, leadership, commitment, strategy, objectives, and activities of the organization.

### Key Statements

- Risk management is embedded in all organizational activities
- Everyone in the organization is responsible for risk management
- Risk management is part of decision-making
- Risk management considers external and internal contexts

## Implementation in AdminSend GmbH

### Integration into Business Processes

| Process | Risk Management Integration |
|---------|----------------------------|
| Strategic planning | Risk consideration in strategic decisions |
| Project management | Risk management in all project phases |
| Operational processes | Risk identification in daily operations |
| Financial planning | Risk-based budgeting |
| Compliance | Risk assessment of regulatory requirements |

### Responsibilities

- **Board**: Overall responsibility for risk management integration
- **Management**: Integration into business areas
- **Leaders**: Risk management in teams
- **Employees**: Risk identification in daily work

## Success Factors

1. **Top Management Support**: Visible leadership support
2. **Clear Responsibilities**: Defined roles and tasks
3. **Common Language**: Shared risk understanding
4. **Process Integration**: Risk management in existing processes
5. **Cultural Anchoring**: Risk awareness in the organization

## Measurement and Monitoring

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Number of integrated risk management processes
- Employee awareness of risk management
- Quality of risk identification in processes
- Effectiveness of risk mitigation measures
