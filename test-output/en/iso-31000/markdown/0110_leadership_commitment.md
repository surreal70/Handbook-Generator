## Purpose

This document describes the requirements for leadership and commitment in risk management according to ISO 31000 Clause 5.3. It defines top management responsibilities for effective risk management.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- AdminSend GmbH
- Board and executive management
- Leaders at all levels
- Risk management officers

## Leadership Responsibility

Top management bears overall responsibility for risk management and must ensure that risk management is integrated into all organizational activities.

### Top Management Commitments

1. **Take Responsibility**
   - Accountability for risk management
   - Demonstrate leadership role
   - Promote risk management culture

2. **Establish Policy and Objectives**
   - Develop risk management policy
   - Define strategic risk objectives
   - Establish risk appetite

3. **Provide Resources**
   - Adequate personnel resources
   - Ensure financial means
   - Provide technical infrastructure

4. **Ensure Integration**
   - Embed risk management in strategy
   - Integrate into business processes
   - Include in decision-making

## Risk Management Policy

### Policy Elements

The risk management policy must contain:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Purpose | Why risk management is conducted |
| Scope | Where risk management is applied |
| Commitment | Top management engagement |
| Responsibilities | Who is responsible for what |
| Resources | What means are provided |
| Review | How the policy is reviewed |

### Policy Communication

The risk management policy must:
- Be communicated to all employees
- Be available to stakeholders
- Be regularly reviewed and updated
- Be integrated into organizational documents

## Risk Management Objectives

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Value Creation**
   - Contribution to objective achievement
   - Exploit opportunities
   - Create competitive advantages

2. **Protection**
   - Protect assets
   - Preserve reputation
   - Ensure compliance

3. **Decision Quality**
   - Informed decisions
   - Create transparency
   - Manage uncertainties

### Operational Objectives

- Identify risks early
- Assess risks appropriately
- Implement effective measures
- Continuously monitor and improve

## Risk Appetite

### Risk Appetite Definition

The organization defines:
- Acceptable risk levels
- Risk tolerances for different areas
- Criteria for risk acceptance
- Escalation thresholds

### Risk Appetite Categories

| Category | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Strategic | Risks to business objectives | Market risks |
| Operational | Risks in daily operations | Process risks |
| Financial | Financial risks | Liquidity risks |
| Compliance | Regulatory risks | Legal changes |

## Resource Provision

### Personnel Resources

- Risk managers and team
- Subject matter experts for risk analysis
- Training and development
- External consultants as needed

### Financial Resources

- Budget for risk management activities
- Funds for risk mitigation measures
- Investments in risk management systems
- Insurance and provisions

### Technical Resources

- Risk management software
- Data analysis tools
- Communication platforms
- Documentation systems

## Leadership Activities

### Regular Activities

1. **Risk Reviews**
   - Quarterly risk assessments
   - Discussion of critical risks
   - Decisions on measures

2. **Strategic Planning**
   - Include risks in strategy development
   - Consider risk scenarios
   - Make risk-based decisions

3. **Communication**
   - Promote risk awareness
   - Communicate successes
   - Create transparency

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| CEO/Managing Director | Overall responsibility for risk management |
| Board | Oversight and strategic direction |
| CFO | Financial risks and resources |
| Department Heads | Risk management in their areas |
| Risk Manager | Coordination and support |

## Success Measurement

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Number of identified risks
- Percentage of treated risks
- Effectiveness of measures
- Risk management maturity
- Employee engagement
