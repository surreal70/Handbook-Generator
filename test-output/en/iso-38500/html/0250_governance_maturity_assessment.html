Purpose

This document describes the assessment of the organization's IT governance maturity.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - Complete IT governance assessment

Maturity Model

Level 1: Initial / Ad-hoc

Characteristics: - No formal IT governance - Reactive decisions - Unclear responsibilities - No documented processes

Level 2: Repeatable

Characteristics: - Basic governance structures - Some documented processes - Defined responsibilities - Project-based management

Level 3: Defined

Characteristics: - Formal governance framework - Documented processes and policies - Clear roles and responsibilities - Standardized procedures

Level 4: Managed

Characteristics: - Measured and controlled processes - KPI-based management - Continuous monitoring - Proactive risk management

Level 5: Optimized

Characteristics: - Continuous improvement - Innovation and optimization - Benchmarking and best practices - Strategic alignment
