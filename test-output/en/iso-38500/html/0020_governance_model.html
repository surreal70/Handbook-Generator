Purpose

This document describes the organization's IT Governance Model based on ISO/IEC 38500. It defines the structure, roles, and processes for effective IT governance.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - All IT governance activities - Board, Executive Management, and IT Management

Governance Model

Organizational Structure

Board
    ↓
Executive Management
    ↓
IT Governance Committee
    ↓
CIO / IT Leadership
    ↓
IT Management

Governance Levels

  1. Strategic Level: Board and Executive Management
  2. Setting IT strategy
  3. Approving IT investments

  4. Monitoring IT performance

  5. Tactical Level: IT Governance Committee and CIO

  6. Implementing IT strategy
  7. IT portfolio management

  8. Risk and compliance management

  9. Operational Level: IT Management

  10. Daily IT operations
  11. Service delivery
  12. Technical implementation

Governance Bodies

IT Governance Committee

Composition: - [TODO] (Chair) - [TODO] - [TODO] - Business Unit Leaders - External advisors (as needed)

Responsibilities: - Oversight of IT strategy - Approval of IT projects - Risk assessment - Compliance monitoring

Meeting Frequency: Quarterly

IT Steering Committee

Composition: - [TODO] (Chair) - IT Department Heads - Project Managers - Business Representatives

Responsibilities: - Project prioritization - Resource allocation - Problem escalation - Performance monitoring

Meeting Frequency: Monthly

Decision Processes

IT Investment Decisions

  1. Application: Create business case
  2. Evaluation: Cost-benefit analysis
  3. Prioritization: Portfolio assessment
  4. Approval: Governance Committee
  5. Monitoring: Regular reviews

Risk Management Decisions

  1. Identification: Recognize risks
  2. Assessment: Analyze risks
  3. Treatment: Define measures
  4. Approval: Risk acceptance
  5. Monitoring: Continuous control

Reporting

To the Board

  • Frequency: Quarterly
  • Content:
  • IT strategy status
  • Major IT projects
  • IT risks
  • IT costs and benefits
  • Compliance status

To Executive Management

  • Frequency: Monthly
  • Content:
  • IT performance metrics
  • Project progress
  • Incidents and problems
  • Resource utilization

Governance Processes

  1. Strategic Planning: Annually
  2. Portfolio Management: Continuous
  3. Risk Management: Continuous
  4. Performance Management: Monthly
  5. Compliance Management: Continuous
