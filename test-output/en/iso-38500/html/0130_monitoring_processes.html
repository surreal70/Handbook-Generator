Purpose

This document describes the monitoring processes (Monitor) within the EDM model.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - All IT monitoring activities

Monitoring Processes

1. Performance Monitoring

Frequency: Continuous

Activities: - Measure KPIs - Analyze trends - Identify deviations - Update dashboards - Create reports

Responsible: IT Management

Output: Performance reports

2. Compliance Monitoring

Frequency: Continuous

Activities: - Check policy compliance - Identify compliance violations - Track corrective actions - Monitor audit findings - Report compliance status

Responsible: Compliance Officer, CISO

Output: Compliance reports

3. Strategy Implementation Monitoring

Frequency: Quarterly

Activities: - Check milestones - Measure goal achievement - Identify obstacles - Recognize adjustment needs - Report progress

Responsible: CIO, PMO

Output: Strategy implementation reports

4. Risk Monitoring

Frequency: Continuous

Activities: - Monitor risk indicators - Identify new risks - Track risk treatment - Assess residual risks - Report risk status

Responsible: Risk Manager, CISO

Output: Risk reports

Monitoring Instruments

Instrument Purpose
Dashboards Real-time visualization
KPIs Performance measurement
Reports Periodic information
Audits Independent review

Reporting

To the Board

  • Frequency: Quarterly
  • Content: Strategic KPIs, risks, compliance

To Executive Management

  • Frequency: Monthly
  • Content: Operational KPIs, projects, incidents
