Governance Forums

Board Level

IT Governance Committee: - Frequency: Quarterly - Participants: Board members, CIO, CTO - Purpose: Strategic oversight, risk review - Outputs: Recommendations, approvals

Executive Level

IT Steering Committee: - Frequency: Monthly - Participants: Executive team, IT leadership - Purpose: Strategy execution, investment decisions - Outputs: Decisions, priorities, resource allocation

Operational Level

IT Management Forum: - Frequency: Weekly - Participants: IT managers, team leads - Purpose: Operations review, issue resolution - Outputs: Action items, escalations
