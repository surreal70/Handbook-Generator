Purpose

This document defines the roles and responsibilities for IT governance in the organization.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - All IT governance roles

Governance Roles

Board

Responsibilities: - Overall responsibility for IT governance - Approval of IT strategy - Setting risk tolerance - Monitoring IT performance - Ensuring compliance

Authorities: - Strategic IT decisions - Budget approvals - CIO appointment - Governance policies

Executive Management

Responsibilities: - Implementation of IT strategy - IT investment decisions - Resource allocation - Risk management - Performance monitoring

Authorities: - Operational IT decisions - Project approvals - Resource allocation - Policy implementation

Chief Information Officer (CIO)

Responsibilities: - IT operations and services - IT strategy development - IT portfolio management - Vendor management - IT governance processes

Authorities: - IT architecture decisions - Technology selection - IT personnel decisions - Service level setting

IT Management

Responsibilities: - Daily IT operations - Service delivery - Technical implementation - Incident management - Change management

Authorities: - Technical decisions - Operational processes - Resource utilization - Problem resolution

IT Governance Committee

Responsibilities: - Oversight of IT strategy - Project prioritization - Risk assessment - Compliance monitoring - Board advisory

Authorities: - Project approvals - Board recommendations - Escalation decisions
