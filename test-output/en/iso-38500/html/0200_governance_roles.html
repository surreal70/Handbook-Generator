Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive overview of all governance roles within the IT governance framework.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - All governance stakeholders - IT governance structure

Role Categories

Strategic Level

Board of Directors: - Ultimate accountability for IT governance - Strategic oversight and direction - Risk appetite definition - Resource allocation approval

Executive Committee: - Strategic implementation - Cross-functional coordination - Performance oversight - Investment prioritization

Operational Level

IT Leadership: - IT strategy execution - Service delivery management - Technology governance - Vendor relationships

Business Unit Leaders: - Business-IT alignment - Requirements definition - Value realization - Change adoption

Support Level

IT Governance Office: - Framework maintenance - Compliance monitoring - Reporting and metrics - Process improvement

Risk and Compliance: - Risk assessment - Regulatory compliance - Audit coordination - Control effectiveness

Role Relationships

Accountability Chain

Board → Executive Management → IT Leadership → IT Teams

Communication Flows

  • Upward: Performance, risks, issues
  • Downward: Strategy, policies, decisions
  • Lateral: Coordination, collaboration
