Purpose

This document describes the evaluation processes (Evaluate) within the EDM model.

Scope

This document applies to: - AdminSend GmbH - All IT evaluation activities

Evaluation Processes

1. Strategic IT Assessment

Frequency: Annually

Activities: - Analyze business strategy - Assess IT capabilities - Evaluate technology trends - Conduct competitive analysis - Identify strategic options

Responsible: Board, Executive Management, CIO

Output: Strategic IT assessment and recommendations

2. IT Risk Assessment

Frequency: Quarterly

Activities: - Identify IT risks - Analyze and assess risks - Review risk tolerance - Evaluate treatment options - Assess residual risks

Responsible: CIO, CISO, Risk Manager

Output: Risk register and treatment plans

3. IT Performance Assessment

Frequency: Monthly

Activities: - Analyze KPIs - Review SLA compliance - Assess user satisfaction - Evaluate cost efficiency - Identify improvement opportunities

Responsible: IT Management

Output: Performance reports and improvement suggestions

4. Technology Assessment

Frequency: Continuous

Activities: - Evaluate new technologies - Review technology roadmap - Assess technical debt - Identify modernization needs - Recognize innovation potential

Responsible: CTO, IT Architects

Output: Technology assessments and recommendations

Assessment Criteria

Area Criteria
Strategy Alignment, relevance, feasibility
Risk Likelihood, impact, controllability
Performance Efficiency, effectiveness, quality
Technology Currency, suitability, future-readiness
