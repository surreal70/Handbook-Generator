## Purpose

This document defines the responsibilities of executive management in implementing and operating IT governance.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- AdminSend GmbH
- Executive management team
- C-level executives

## Executive Roles

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

**Strategic Leadership:**
- Overall accountability for IT governance
- Business-IT alignment
- Digital transformation leadership
- Culture and values

**Decision Authority:**
- IT strategy approval (recommendation to Board)
- Executive appointments
- Major initiatives
- Resource allocation

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

**Financial Governance:**
- IT budget management
- Investment evaluation
- Cost optimization
- Financial reporting

**Value Management:**
- ROI measurement
- Business case validation
- Portfolio optimization
- Value realization

### Chief Information Officer (CIO)

**IT Leadership:**
- IT strategy development
- Technology governance
- Service delivery
- Innovation leadership

**Operational Management:**
- IT operations oversight
- Vendor management
- Architecture governance
- Capability development

### Chief Operating Officer (COO)

**Operational Integration:**
- Business process integration
- Service delivery alignment
- Operational efficiency
- Change management

**Performance Management:**
- Service level monitoring
- Operational metrics
- Process optimization
- Continuous improvement

### Chief Risk Officer (CRO)

**Risk Management:**
- IT risk assessment
- Risk mitigation oversight
- Compliance monitoring
- Control effectiveness

**Governance Oversight:**
- Policy compliance
- Regulatory requirements
- Audit coordination
- Risk reporting

## Collective Responsibilities

### Executive Committee

**Strategic Decisions:**
- IT strategy review and approval
- Major investment decisions
- Risk appetite setting
- Resource prioritization

**Governance Activities:**
- Policy approval
- Performance review
- Issue resolution
- Stakeholder communication

## Implementation Responsibilities

### Strategy Execution

**Planning:**
- Translate strategy to initiatives
- Define success criteria
- Allocate resources
- Set timelines

**Execution:**
- Project oversight
- Progress monitoring
- Issue escalation
- Benefit realization

### Resource Management

**Budget Management:**
- Annual budget planning
- Quarterly reviews
- Variance analysis
- Reallocation decisions

**Talent Management:**
- IT leadership development
- Succession planning
- Skills assessment
- Recruitment oversight

### Risk Management

**Risk Oversight:**
- Risk identification
- Mitigation planning
- Control implementation
- Monitoring effectiveness

**Incident Management:**
- Major incident oversight
- Crisis management
- Communication coordination
- Recovery validation

## Performance Management

### Metrics and KPIs

**Strategic Metrics:**
- Business value delivered
- Strategic initiative progress
- Innovation metrics
- Stakeholder satisfaction

**Operational Metrics:**
- Service availability
- Project delivery
- Cost efficiency
- Security posture

### Review Processes

**Monthly Reviews:**
- Performance dashboard
- Project status
- Risk updates
- Budget variance

**Quarterly Reviews:**
- Strategic progress
- Portfolio review
- Capability assessment
- Governance effectiveness

## Decision-Making Framework

### Decision Types

**Strategic Decisions:**
- Require Board approval
- Executive committee review
- Comprehensive analysis
- Stakeholder consultation

**Tactical Decisions:**
- Executive authority
- CIO recommendation
- Business case required
- Risk assessment

**Operational Decisions:**
- Delegated to IT management
- Within approved budget
- Standard processes
- Regular reporting

### Escalation Criteria

**Escalate to Board:**
- Strategic changes
- Major investments
- Significant risks
- Regulatory issues

**Executive Resolution:**
- Cross-functional conflicts
- Resource constraints
- Priority changes
- Policy exceptions

## Communication Responsibilities

### Upward Communication

**To Board:**
- Strategic recommendations
- Performance reports
- Risk updates
- Compliance status

### Downward Communication

**To Organization:**
- Strategic direction
- Priorities and goals
- Policy changes
- Performance expectations

### Lateral Communication

**Peer Coordination:**
- Cross-functional alignment
- Resource sharing
- Issue resolution
- Best practice sharing
