## Purpose

This document describes the organization's IT Governance Model based on ISO/IEC 38500. It defines the structure, roles, and processes for effective IT governance.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- AdminSend GmbH
- All IT governance activities
- Board, Executive Management, and IT Management

## Governance Model

### Organizational Structure

```
Board
    ↓
Executive Management
    ↓
IT Governance Committee
    ↓
CIO / IT Leadership
    ↓
IT Management
```

### Governance Levels

1. **Strategic Level**: Board and Executive Management
   - Setting IT strategy
   - Approving IT investments
   - Monitoring IT performance

2. **Tactical Level**: IT Governance Committee and CIO
   - Implementing IT strategy
   - IT portfolio management
   - Risk and compliance management

3. **Operational Level**: IT Management
   - Daily IT operations
   - Service delivery
   - Technical implementation

## Decision Processes

### IT Investment Decisions

1. **Application**: Create business case
2. **Evaluation**: Cost-benefit analysis
3. **Prioritization**: Portfolio assessment
4. **Approval**: Governance Committee
5. **Monitoring**: Regular reviews

### Risk Management Decisions

1. **Identification**: Recognize risks
2. **Assessment**: Analyze risks
3. **Treatment**: Define measures
4. **Approval**: Risk acceptance
5. **Monitoring**: Continuous control

## Reporting

### To the Board

- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**: 
  - IT strategy status
  - Major IT projects
  - IT risks
  - IT costs and benefits
  - Compliance status

### To Executive Management

- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Content**:
  - IT performance metrics
  - Project progress
  - Incidents and problems
  - Resource utilization
