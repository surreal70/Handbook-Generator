This document describes the organization's policies, procedures, and practices for promoting integrity and ethical values in accordance with COSO Principle 1 and Trust Services Criterion CC1.1.

Scope

This document covers: - Code of conduct and ethical standards - Communication of ethical expectations - Enforcement of ethical standards - Conflicts of interest - Whistleblower mechanisms

Overview

Integrity and ethical values are essential elements of the control environment and influence the design, administration, and monitoring of other components of internal control. The organization demonstrates a commitment to integrity and ethical values through established standards and enforcement mechanisms.
