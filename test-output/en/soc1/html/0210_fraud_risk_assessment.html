Purpose

This document describes the process for identifying, assessing, and responding to fraud risks in accordance with the COSO Internal Control Framework and Trust Services Criteria CC2.

Scope

This document covers: - Fraud risk identification - Fraud risk assessment - Fraud risk response - Fraud monitoring and detection - Reporting and investigation

Overview

The organization assesses the potential for fraud in achieving its objectives. A comprehensive fraud risk assessment is critical for developing effective controls to prevent, detect, and respond to fraud.
