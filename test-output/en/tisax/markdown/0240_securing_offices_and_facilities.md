## Purpose

This document defines measures for securing offices and facilities according to TISAX requirements.

## Scope

This document applies to all offices and facilities of [TODO].

## Office Security

### Clean Desk Policy
- No confidential documents on desks
- Locking cabinets and drawers
- Logging out of computers when absent
- Secure storage of data carriers

### Clear Screen Policy
- Screen lock when absent
- Automatic screen lock after [TODO] minutes
- No viewing from outside possible
- Privacy screens if needed

### Document Security
- Classification of all documents
- Secure storage
- Controlled destruction
- Logging of sensitive documents

## Facility Security

### Server Rooms
- Separate access control
- Air conditioning
- Fire protection
- Monitoring
- Redundant power supply

### Meeting Rooms
- Privacy protection for confidential meetings
- No unattended documents
- Cleaning after meetings
- Technical eavesdropping protection if needed

### Archive Rooms
- Access control
- Fire protection
- Climate control
- Inventory management

## Environmental Security

### Fire Protection
- Fire alarm system
- Fire extinguishers
- Sprinkler system
- Regular fire drills

### Water Protection
- Water sensors in critical areas
- Regular inspections
- Emergency plans

### Air Conditioning
- Temperature control
- Humidity control
- Redundant systems
- Monitoring and alerting

## TISAX-Specific Requirements

### VDA ISA Controls
- **5.3**: Securing offices, rooms and facilities

### Assessment Evidence
- Security policies
- Training evidence
- Audit reports
