TISAX Overview

What is TISAX?

TISAX is an information security assessment standard specifically developed for the automotive industry. It is based on the VDA ISA catalog (Verband der Automobilindustrie Information Security Assessment) and enables standardized exchange of assessment results between automotive manufacturers and their suppliers.

Main Objectives

  • Standardized assessment of information security
  • Mutual recognition of assessment results
  • Reduction of multiple assessments
  • Protection of sensitive information in the automotive industry

Assessment Areas

  1. Information Security: Protection of information and IT systems
  2. Prototype Protection: Protection of prototypes and development information
  3. Data Protection: Protection of personal data

Assessment Levels

  • AL1: Basic assessment
  • AL2: Detailed assessment with on-site audit
  • AL3: Very detailed assessment with comprehensive on-site audit
