## Prioritization and Assessment

### Assessment Criteria

Interested parties are assessed based on:
1. **Influence:** How strongly can the party influence the QMS?
2. **Dependency:** How dependent is the organization on this party?
3. **Impact:** What impact do the requirements have on the QMS?
4. **Urgency:** How urgently must the requirements be met?

### Prioritization Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Actions |
|----------|----------|---------|
| **Critical** | High influence + High dependency | Intensive monitoring, proactive management |
| **Important** | Medium influence or medium dependency | Regular monitoring, reactive management |
| **Normal** | Low influence + Low dependency | Periodic review |
