Prioritization and Assessment

Assessment Criteria

Interested parties are assessed based on: 1. Influence: How strongly can the party influence the QMS? 2. Dependency: How dependent is the organization on this party? 3. Impact: What impact do the requirements have on the QMS? 4. Urgency: How urgently must the requirements be met?
