Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive mapping between the GDPR compliance templates and the specific articles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR - EU 2016/679). It serves as a reference guide to understand which templates address which legal requirements and helps ensure complete coverage of GDPR obligations.

How to Use This Mapping

  • For Compliance: Verify complete coverage of all relevant GDPR requirements

Coverage Analysis

Complete Coverage

The following GDPR chapters are comprehensively covered by templates:

  • ✅ Chapter I (Art. 1-4): General Provisions
  • ✅ Chapter II (Art. 5-11): Principles
  • ✅ Chapter III (Art. 12-23): Rights of the Data Subject
  • ✅ Chapter IV (Art. 24-43): Controller and Processor
  • ✅ Chapter V (Art. 44-50): Transfers to Third Countries

Partial Coverage

The following chapters are partially covered (primarily through reference):

  • ⚠️ Chapter VI (Art. 51-59): Independent Supervisory Authorities (interaction covered in templates)
  • ⚠️ Chapter VII (Art. 60-76): Cooperation and Consistency (relevant for cross-border processing)
  • ⚠️ Chapter VIII (Art. 77-84): Remedies, Liability and Penalties (rights information covered)

Not Covered

The following chapters are not covered as they address regulatory framework rather than organizational implementation:

  • ❌ Chapter IX (Art. 85-91): Provisions Relating to Specific Processing Situations
  • ❌ Chapter X (Art. 92-93): Delegated Acts and Implementing Acts
  • ❌ Chapter XI (Art. 94-99): Final Provisions

Usage Recommendations

For New Implementations

  1. Start with Foundation Templates (0010-0050) to establish basic framework
  2. Implement Core Compliance Requirements (Principles, Lawfulness, Accountability)
  3. Set up Data Subject Rights processes (0200-0250)
  4. Establish Security and Risk Management (0150, 0330, 0400)
  5. Manage Processors appropriately (0310, 0720)
  6. Address International Transfers if applicable (0500, 0510)

For Audits and Reviews

  1. Use this mapping to verify complete coverage of applicable GDPR articles
  2. Cross-reference implemented templates with organizational processing activities
  3. Identify gaps where templates exist but are not implemented
  4. Document deviations and justifications

For Specific Processing Activities

  1. Identify applicable GDPR articles for the processing
  2. Use this mapping to find relevant templates
  3. Customize templates to specific processing context
  4. Document implementation in Records of Processing Activities (Art. 30)
