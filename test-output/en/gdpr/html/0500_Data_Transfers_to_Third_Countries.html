Purpose

This document regulates the transfer of personal data to third countries (outside EU/EEA) at AdminSend GmbH according to Art. 44-50 GDPR. It ensures that an adequate level of protection is guaranteed even for international data transfers.

Principle (Art. 44)

A transfer of personal data to a third country may only take place if: - The conditions of Art. 45-49 are complied with - The other provisions of GDPR are complied with - Including data subject rights and remedies

Appropriate Safeguards (Art. 46)

Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA)

Requirement

Following the Schrems-II judgment (CJEU C-311/18), the controller must assess whether the level of protection in the third country is equivalent to that in the EU.

TIA Required for: - All transfers based on Art. 46 (safeguards) - Particularly for transfers to USA and other countries with government access possibilities

Derogations (Art. 49)

Without an adequacy decision or appropriate safeguards, a transfer is only permissible in exceptional cases.
