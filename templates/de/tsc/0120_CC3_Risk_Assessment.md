## 1. CC3.1: Risk Identification

**Control Activities:**
- Annual risk assessment conducted
- Risk identification process
- Threat and vulnerability analysis
- Risk register maintained

## 2. CC3.2: Risk Analysis

**Control Activities:**
- Risk likelihood and impact assessed
- Risk prioritization
- Risk treatment options evaluated
- Risk acceptance criteria defined

## 3. CC3.3: Risk Response

**Control Activities:**
- Risk treatment plans developed
- Controls implemented
- Residual risk monitored
- Risk reporting to management
