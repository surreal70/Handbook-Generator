**Control Activities:**
- Code of Conduct established and communicated
- Annual acknowledgment by all employees

## 4. CC1.3: Management Oversight

**Control Objective:** Management establishes oversight responsibilities.

**Control Activities:**
- Organizational structure defined
- Reporting lines established
- Authority and responsibility assigned
- Performance evaluations conducted

## 5. CC1.4: Competence

**Control Objective:** The entity demonstrates a commitment to attract, develop, and retain competent individuals.

**Control Activities:**
- Job descriptions defined
- Hiring process includes background checks
- Training programs established
- Performance reviews conducted

## 6. CC1.5: Accountability

**Control Objective:** The entity holds individuals accountable for their responsibilities.

**Control Activities:**
- Performance metrics defined
- Regular performance reviews
- Consequences for non-performance
- Rewards for good performance
