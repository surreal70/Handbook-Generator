## ITIL v4 Service Value System

Die Templates unterstützen die ITIL v4 Service Value Chain:

- **Plan**: Capacity Management, Availability Management
- **Improve**: Problem Management, Knowledge Management
- **Engage**: Service Level Management, Service Desk
- **Design & Transition**: Change Management, Release Management
- **Obtain/Build**: Configuration Management, Asset Management
- **Deliver & Support**: Incident Management, Monitoring
