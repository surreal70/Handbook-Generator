# TISAX Framework Mapping

## Overview

This document maps the handbook templates to specific TISAX assessment objectives and VDA ISA controls.

## TISAX Assessment Areas

TISAX is based on the VDA ISA catalog and comprises three main areas:

1. **Information Security**
2. **Prototype Protection**
3. **Data Protection**

## Assessment Level Mapping

### AL1 (Basic Assessment)
All templates are relevant for AL1, focusing on policies and basic processes.

### AL2 (Detailed Assessment)
All templates with detailed documentation of implementation and evidence.

### AL3 (Very Detailed Assessment)
All templates with comprehensive documentation, including detailed technical implementation and evidence.

## Notes

- This mapping serves as a guide and may need to be adapted to specific TISAX assessment requirements
- Consult the current VDA ISA catalog for detailed control requirements
- Some controls may require multiple templates
- Additional organization-specific documentation may be required
