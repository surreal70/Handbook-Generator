## Document Purpose

This handbook documents IT governance in accordance with ISO/IEC 38500. It serves as a guide for implementing and managing IT governance practices based on the six principles: Responsibility, Strategy, Acquisition, Performance, Conformance, and Human Behavior. The handbook supports the Evaluate-Direct-Monitor (EDM) model.
