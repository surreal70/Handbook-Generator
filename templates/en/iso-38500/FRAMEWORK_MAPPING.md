# ISO/IEC 38500 Framework Mapping

## Overview

This document maps the handbook templates to ISO/IEC 38500 principles and practices.

## 1. Governance Framework and Principles (0010-0099)

### Governance Framework
**Templates:**
- 0010_governance_framework.md - IT Governance Framework Overview
- 0020_governance_model.md - IT Governance Model
- 0030_principles_overview.md - Overview of Six Principles

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Chapter 4: Model for corporate governance of IT
- Chapter 5: Guiding principles
- Defines the foundations of IT governance

### Principle 1: Responsibility
**Template:**
- 0040_responsibility_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 1: Responsibility
- Individuals and groups understand and accept their responsibilities
- Those with responsibility have the authority to fulfill it

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Assess responsibilities
- Direct: Assign responsibilities
- Monitor: Monitor fulfillment

### Principle 2: Strategy
**Template:**
- 0050_strategy_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 2: Strategy
- IT strategy considers current and future IT capabilities
- IT strategy meets current and ongoing needs of business strategy

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Review strategic alignment
- Direct: Communicate strategy
- Monitor: Monitor strategy implementation

### Principle 3: Acquisition
**Template:**
- 0060_acquisition_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 3: Acquisition
- IT acquisitions are made for valid reasons
- Based on appropriate analysis with clear decisions
- Appropriate balance between benefits, opportunities, costs, and risks

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Assess acquisition needs
- Direct: Direct acquisition process
- Monitor: Monitor acquisition results

### Principle 4: Performance
**Template:**
- 0070_performance_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 4: Performance
- IT is fit for purpose to support the organization
- Provides required services, service levels, and service quality

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Assess performance requirements
- Direct: Set performance targets
- Monitor: Measure performance

### Principle 5: Conformance
**Template:**
- 0080_conformance_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 5: Conformance
- IT complies with all mandatory legislation and regulations
- Policies and practices are clearly defined, implemented, and enforced

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Assess compliance requirements
- Direct: Direct compliance measures
- Monitor: Monitor compliance

### Principle 6: Human Behavior
**Template:**
- 0090_human_behavior_principle.md

**ISO/IEC 38500 Reference:**
- Principle 6: Human Behavior
- IT policies, practices, and decisions demonstrate respect for human behavior
- Consideration of current and evolving needs of all people in the process

**Practices:**
- Evaluate: Assess impact on people
- Direct: Promote human-centered approaches
- Monitor: Monitor satisfaction
