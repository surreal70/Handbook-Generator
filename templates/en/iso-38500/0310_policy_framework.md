## Core Governance Policies

### IT Governance Policy

**Purpose:**
- Establish governance framework
- Define governance structure
- Assign responsibilities
- Set governance principles

**Scope:**
- All IT activities
- All IT assets
- All IT stakeholders
- All IT investments

**Key Requirements:**
- Governance structure
- Decision rights
- Accountability framework
- Review processes

### IT Strategy Policy

**Purpose:**
- Guide IT strategy development
- Ensure business alignment
- Define strategic planning process
- Set strategic objectives

**Key Requirements:**
- Strategy development process
- Alignment mechanisms
- Review and approval
- Communication requirements

### Investment Governance Policy

**Purpose:**
- Govern IT investments
- Ensure value delivery
- Manage portfolio
- Control spending

**Key Requirements:**
- Investment approval process
- Business case requirements
- Portfolio management
- Benefit realization

### Risk Management Policy

**Purpose:**
- Manage IT risks
- Define risk appetite
- Establish risk processes
- Ensure risk awareness

**Key Requirements:**
- Risk assessment process
- Risk treatment approach
- Risk monitoring
- Risk reporting

### Security Policy

**Purpose:**
- Protect IT assets
- Ensure confidentiality
- Maintain integrity
- Ensure availability

**Key Requirements:**
- Security controls
- Access management
- Incident response
- Security awareness

### Data Governance Policy

**Purpose:**
- Govern data assets
- Ensure data quality
- Protect data privacy
- Enable data value

**Key Requirements:**
- Data ownership
- Data quality standards
- Privacy protection
- Data lifecycle management

### Compliance Policy

**Purpose:**

- Maintain certifications
- Manage audits

**Key Requirements:**
- Compliance framework
- Control requirements
- Audit processes
- Remediation procedures

## Policy Development Process

### Policy Lifecycle

**1. Initiation:**
- Identify need
- Define scope
- Assign ownership
- Establish timeline

**2. Development:**
- Research requirements
- Draft policy
- Stakeholder consultation
- Legal review

**3. Approval:**
- Management review
- Executive approval
- Board approval (if required)
- Publication

**4. Implementation:**
- Communication
- Training
- Tool support
- Compliance monitoring

**5. Review and Update:**
- Periodic review
- Change management
- Version control
- Archive management
