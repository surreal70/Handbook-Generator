## Purpose

This document defines the specific responsibilities of the Board of Directors in IT governance according to ISO 38500.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- {{ meta-organisation.name }}
- Board of Directors
- Board committees with IT oversight

## Primary Responsibilities

### Strategic Oversight

**IT Strategy Approval:**
- Review and approve IT strategy
- Ensure alignment with business strategy
- Validate strategic objectives
- Monitor strategy execution

**Investment Governance:**
- Approve major IT investments
- Review business cases
- Monitor return on investment
- Ensure value realization

### Risk Governance

**Risk Oversight:**
- Define risk appetite for IT
- Review significant IT risks
- Ensure risk mitigation
- Monitor risk exposure

**Cybersecurity Oversight:**
- Understand cyber threats
- Review security posture
- Ensure incident response capability
- Monitor security metrics

### Performance Monitoring

**Performance Review:**
- Review IT performance metrics
- Assess service delivery
- Monitor project outcomes
- Evaluate IT leadership

**Compliance Oversight:**
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Review audit findings
- Monitor control effectiveness
- Approve compliance policies

## Board Committees

### IT Governance Committee

**Composition:**
- Board members with IT expertise
- Independent advisors
- Executive management (ex-officio)

**Responsibilities:**
- Detailed IT oversight
- Strategy review
- Risk assessment
- Board recommendations

### Audit Committee

**IT-Related Duties:**
- IT control review
- Cybersecurity oversight
- IT audit coordination
- Compliance monitoring

## Decision-Making Authority

### Approval Authority

**Strategic Decisions:**
- IT strategy and roadmap
- Major technology investments (>{{ meta-handbook.investment_threshold }})
- IT organizational changes
- Significant vendor contracts

**Policy Approval:**
- IT governance policies
- Risk management policies
- Security policies
- Compliance frameworks

### Delegation

**Delegated to Executive Management:**
- Operational IT decisions
- Tactical investments
- Day-to-day management
- Vendor selection (below threshold)

## Reporting Requirements

### Regular Reports

**Quarterly:**
- IT performance dashboard
- Project portfolio status
- Risk register review
- Compliance status

**Annual:**
- IT strategy review
- Budget approval
- Audit results
- Governance effectiveness

### Ad-Hoc Reports

**Immediate Notification:**
- Major security incidents
- Significant project failures
- Regulatory violations
- Critical system outages

## Board Competencies

### Required Knowledge

**Technical Understanding:**
- IT fundamentals
- Cybersecurity basics
- Digital transformation
- Emerging technologies

**Governance Knowledge:**
- ISO 38500 principles
- Risk management
- Compliance requirements
- Industry best practices

### Continuous Education

**Board Development:**
- Regular IT briefings
- Industry conferences
- Expert presentations
- Site visits
