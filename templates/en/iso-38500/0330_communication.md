## Communication Plans

### Regular Communications

**Board Communications:**
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Format:** Executive presentation
- **Content:** Strategic progress, risks, compliance
- **Owner:** CIO
- **Approval:** Executive Committee

**Executive Dashboard:**
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Format:** Visual dashboard
- **Content:** KPIs, project status, risks
- **Owner:** Governance Office
- **Distribution:** Executive team

**Business Updates:**
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Format:** Newsletter
- **Content:** Service updates, changes, projects
- **Owner:** Service Management
- **Distribution:** All staff

**IT Team Updates:**
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Format:** Team meeting
- **Content:** Priorities, issues, achievements
- **Owner:** IT Management
- **Distribution:** IT teams

### Event-Driven Communications

**Major Incidents:**
- **Trigger:** Severity 1-2 incidents
- **Timing:** Immediate
- **Content:** Impact, status, actions
- **Audience:** Affected stakeholders
- **Owner:** Incident Manager

**Strategic Changes:**
- **Trigger:** Strategy updates
- **Timing:** Advance notice
- **Content:** Changes, rationale, impact
- **Audience:** All stakeholders
- **Owner:** CIO

**Policy Changes:**
- **Trigger:** Policy updates
- **Timing:** Before implementation
- **Content:** Changes, requirements, timeline
- **Audience:** Affected parties
- **Owner:** Policy Owner

**Project Milestones:**
- **Trigger:** Major milestones
- **Timing:** At completion
- **Content:** Achievement, next steps, benefits
- **Audience:** Project stakeholders
- **Owner:** Project Manager
