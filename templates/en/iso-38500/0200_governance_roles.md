## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive overview of all governance roles within the IT governance framework.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- {{ meta-organisation.name }}
- All governance stakeholders
- IT governance structure

## Role Categories

### Strategic Level

**Board of Directors:**
- Ultimate accountability for IT governance
- Strategic oversight and direction
- Risk appetite definition
- Resource allocation approval

**Executive Committee:**
- Strategic implementation
- Cross-functional coordination
- Performance oversight
- Investment prioritization

### Operational Level

**IT Leadership:**
- IT strategy execution
- Service delivery management
- Technology governance
- Vendor relationships

**Business Unit Leaders:**
- Business-IT alignment
- Requirements definition
- Value realization
- Change adoption

### Support Level

**IT Governance Office:**
- Framework maintenance
- Compliance monitoring
- Reporting and metrics
- Process improvement

**Risk and Compliance:**
- Risk assessment
- Regulatory compliance
- Audit coordination
- Control effectiveness

## Role Relationships

### Accountability Chain

```
Board → Executive Management → IT Leadership → IT Teams
```

### Communication Flows

- Upward: Performance, risks, issues
- Downward: Strategy, policies, decisions
- Lateral: Coordination, collaboration

## RACI Matrix

| Activity | Board | Executive | CIO | IT Mgmt | Business |
|----------|-------|-----------|-----|---------|----------|
| IT Strategy | A | R | C | I | C |
| Investment Decisions | A | R | C | I | C |
| Risk Management | A | R | R | C | C |
| Service Delivery | I | A | R | R | C |
| Compliance | A | R | R | C | I |

**Legend:**
- R = Responsible
- A = Accountable

- I = Informed
