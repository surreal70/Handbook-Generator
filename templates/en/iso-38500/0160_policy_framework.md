# Policy Framework

## Purpose

This document describes the policy framework for IT governance.

## Scope

This document applies to:
- {{ meta.organization }}
- All IT policies

## Policy Hierarchy

### Level 1: Governance Policies

- IT Governance Policy
- IT Strategy Policy
- Risk Management Policy

### Level 2: Management Policies

- IT Security Policy
- Data Protection Policy
- Change Management Policy
- Incident Management Policy

### Level 3: Operational Policies

- Usage Policies
- Access Policies
- Backup Policies
- Network Policies

## Policy Lifecycle

### 1. Development

- Identify need
- Draft policy
- Involve stakeholders
- Conduct review

### 2. Approval

- Compliance review
- Management approval
- Board approval (if needed)

### 3. Communication

- Publish policy
- Conduct training
- Create awareness

### 4. Implementation

- Implementation
- Monitoring
- Enforcement

### 5. Review and Update

- Annual review
- Update as needed
- Communicate changes
