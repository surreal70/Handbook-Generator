# Board Oversight

## Purpose

This document describes the structure, responsibilities, and activities of the board of directors regarding oversight of internal control in accordance with COSO Principle 2 and Trust Services Criterion CC1.2.

## Scope

This document covers:
- Board structure and composition
- Board independence
- Oversight functions
- Board committees
- Board activities and meetings

## Overview

The board of directors demonstrates independence from management and exercises oversight over the development and performance of internal control. Effective board oversight is critical to the integrity of the internal control system and stakeholder confidence.
