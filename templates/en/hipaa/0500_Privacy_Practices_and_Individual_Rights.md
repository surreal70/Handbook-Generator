## 1. Purpose

This document describes the privacy practices and procedures for managing individual rights under the HIPAA Privacy Rule for {{ meta-organisation.name }}.

### 1.1 HIPAA Requirements

**Privacy Rule Requirements:**
- Notice of Privacy Practices (§164.520)
- Right of Access (§164.524)
- Right to Amend (§164.526)
- Accounting of Disclosures (§164.528)
- Right to Request Restrictions (§164.522(a))
- Right to Confidential Communications (§164.522(b))

## 2. Notice of Privacy Practices

### 2.1 Notice Requirements

**Content Requirements:**
- Uses and disclosures of PHI
- Individual rights
- Covered entity duties
- Complaint procedures
- Contact information
- Effective date

**Distribution:**
- First service delivery
- Upon request
- Posted prominently
- Available on website
- Material changes require new notice
