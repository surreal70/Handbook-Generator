## Document Purpose

This handbook documents risk management in accordance with ISO 31000. It serves as a guide for implementing and managing risk management practices based on the eight principles: Integrated, Structured and comprehensive, Customized, Inclusive, Dynamic, Best available information, Human and cultural factors, and Continual improvement. The handbook supports the risk management framework and risk management process.
