# ISO 31000 Framework Mapping

## Overview

This document maps the handbook templates to ISO 31000 components and clauses.

## 1. Risk Management Principles (0010-0099)

### Risk Management Overview
**Template:**
- 0010_risk_management_overview.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Chapter 4: Principles
- Chapter 5: Framework
- Chapter 6: Process
- Defines the fundamentals of risk management

### Principle 1: Integrated
**Template:**
- 0020_integrated_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.1: Integrated
- Risk management is an integral part of all organizational activities
- Part of purposes, governance, leadership, commitment, strategy, objectives, and activities

### Principle 2: Structured and comprehensive
**Template:**
- 0030_structured_comprehensive_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.2: Structured and comprehensive
- Systematic, timely, and structured approach
- Contributes to efficiency and consistent, comparable, and reliable results

### Principle 3: Customized
**Template:**
- 0040_customized_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.3: Customized
- Framework and process are customized and proportionate to external and internal context
- Consideration of organizational objectives

### Principle 4 & 5: Inclusive and Dynamic
**Template:**
- 0050_inclusive_dynamic_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.4: Inclusive - Appropriate and timely involvement of stakeholders
- Principle 4.5: Dynamic - Risk management anticipates, detects, and responds to changes

### Principle 6: Best available information
**Template:**
- 0060_best_information_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.6: Best available information
- Inputs based on historical and current information as well as future expectations
- Consideration of limitations and uncertainties

### Principle 7: Human and cultural factors
**Template:**
- 0070_human_cultural_factors_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.7: Human and cultural factors
- Human behavior and culture influence all aspects of risk management
- At each level and stage

### Principle 8: Continual improvement
**Template:**
- 0080_continual_improvement_principle.md

**ISO 31000 Reference:**
- Principle 4.8: Continual improvement
- Risk management is continually improved through learning and experience
- Development of risk management maturity
