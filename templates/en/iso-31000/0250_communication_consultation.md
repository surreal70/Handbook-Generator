## Purpose

This document describes communication and consultation in risk management.

## Communication Objectives

- Promote risk awareness
- Create shared risk understanding
- Engage stakeholders
- Ensure transparency
- Support decision-making

## Communication Channels

| Target Group | Channel | Frequency |
|--------------|---------|-----------|
| Board | Risk report | Quarterly |
| Management | Management meeting | Monthly |
| Employees | Intranet, newsletter | Ongoing |
| External stakeholders | Annual report | Annually |

## Consultation Process

- Stakeholder identification
- Involvement in risk identification
- Feedback on treatment measures
- Participation in decisions
