## Purpose

This document describes documentation and reporting in risk management.

## Risk Register

The risk register contains:
- Risk ID and description
- Category and causes
- Assessment (likelihood, impact)
- Treatment status
- Responsible person
- Last update

## Reporting

### Risk Reports

**Quarterly Reports**:
- Top risks
- New risks
- Treatment progress
- Risk trends

**Ad-hoc Reports**:
- Critical risk events
- Significant changes in risk profile

## Documentation

- Risk management policies
- Process descriptions
- Assessment methods
- Treatment plans
- Lessons learned
