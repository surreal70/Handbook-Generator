## Purpose

This document describes the monitoring of risk management performance.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Framework KPIs

- Risk management maturity level
- Coverage of organization by risk management
- Stakeholder satisfaction
- Resource efficiency

### Process KPIs

- Number of identified risks
- Average assessment time
- Percentage of treated risks
- Average treatment time

### Outcome KPIs

- Number of realized risks
- Cost of risk events
- Reduction of overall risk
- Avoided damages

## Monitoring Methods

- Dashboard reporting
- Trend analyses
- Benchmarking
- Audits and assessments

## Reporting

Monthly KPI reports to:
- Risk management committee
- Management
- Department heads
