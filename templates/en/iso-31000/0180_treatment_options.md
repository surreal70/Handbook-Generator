## Purpose

This document describes the available options for risk treatment.

## Treatment Strategies

### 1. Risk Avoidance

**Description**: Decision not to start or continue an activity

**Application**:
- Risk significantly exceeds risk appetite
- No acceptable treatment measures available
- Activity is not business-critical

### 2. Risk Reduction

**Description**: Actions to reduce likelihood or impact

**Measure Types**:
- Preventive controls (reduce likelihood)
- Detective controls (early detection)
- Corrective controls (reduce impact)

### 3. Risk Transfer

**Description**: Share risk with others

**Methods**:
- Insurance
- Outsourcing
- Contracts and warranties
- Joint ventures

### 4. Risk Acceptance

**Description**: Conscious decision to retain the risk

**Prerequisites**:
- Risk is within risk appetite
- Treatment costs exceed benefits
- No practicable treatment options
