## Purpose

This document describes establishing the context for risk assessment.

## External Context

- Regulatory environment
- Market conditions
- Technological developments
- Social and cultural factors
- Competitive situation

## Internal Context

- Organizational structure
- Governance and decision processes
- Resources and capabilities
- Information systems
- Corporate culture

## Risk Criteria

### Assessment Criteria

- Nature of impacts
- Measurement of impacts
- Timeframe
- Determination of risk level
- Acceptance criteria
