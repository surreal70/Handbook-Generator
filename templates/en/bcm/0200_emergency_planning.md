# 2. Emergency Planning

## 2.1 Critical Business Processes

The following processes have been identified as business-critical:
- Customer service and support
- Production systems
- Financial processing

## 2.2 Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)

- Critical systems: RTO < 4 hours
- Important systems: RTO < 24 hours
- Normal systems: RTO < 72 hours
