# ISO 9001:2015 Framework Mapping

## Overview

This document maps the relationship between the ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System templates and the specific clauses and requirements of the ISO 9001:2015 standard. It serves as a reference for auditors, quality managers, and implementation teams to ensure that all standard requirements are covered by appropriate documentation.

## ISO 9001:2015 Standard Structure

ISO 9001:2015 follows the High-Level Structure (HLS) common to all ISO management system standards. The standard is organized into 10 clauses, with clauses 4-10 containing the main requirements:

- **Clauses 1-3:** Introduction, Scope, Normative References, Terms and Definitions
- **Clause 4:** Context of the Organization
- **Clause 5:** Leadership
- **Clause 6:** Planning
- **Clause 7:** Support
- **Clause 8:** Operation
- **Clause 9:** Performance Evaluation
- **Clause 10:** Improvement
