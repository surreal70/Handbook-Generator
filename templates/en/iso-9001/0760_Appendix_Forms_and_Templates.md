**General Rules:**
- Complete all mandatory fields
- Fill out legibly and completely
- Date in format DD.MM.YYYY
- Signatures where required
- Distribute copies to relevant persons

### Form Release

**Before Use:**
- Form must be approved
- Use current version
- Remove obsolete versions

### Form Retention

**Completed Forms:**
- Retain according to retention periods
- Store securely
- Accessible to authorized persons
- Protect from unauthorized access
