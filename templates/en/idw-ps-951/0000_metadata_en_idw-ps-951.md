## Document Purpose

This handbook documents IT audits in accordance with IDW Auditing Standard 951 "Principles for the Proper Auditing of Compliance Management Systems". It serves as a guide for the systematic audit of IT systems, IT processes, and IT controls in the context of annual financial statement audits and other business audits.
