### 2.3 Assessment Procedures

**Assessment Steps:**
1. **Preparation**
   - Review system documentation
   - Identify assessment objectives
   - Prepare assessment tools

2. **Execution**
   - Conduct examinations
   - Perform interviews
   - Execute tests
   - Document findings

3. **Analysis**
   - Analyze results
   - Identify deficiencies
   - Determine risk
   - Develop recommendations

4. **Reporting**
   - Prepare assessment report
   - Document findings
   - Provide recommendations
   - Brief stakeholders
